Associated Press

Perk Mobile, a great NYC/Bay Area software development company, was working with the Associated Press on a potential desktop app called the "Netbook." They asked if I could whip up a couple concepts to best view categorized and rated articles, plus galleries. This was my first foray into editorial design, and a project I'm proud of considering this was pre-iPad.

Similar work: Chicago Conservation Corps, DEFY Media, Morgan Stanley, The Daily Show with Jon Stewart, XFinity
