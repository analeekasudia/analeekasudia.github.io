Strala Yoga

Tara Stiles is a yoga instructor, founder of Strala Yoga, and personal trainer to Deepak Chopra. She was looking for a revamped personal site/blog and company website. Desired aesthetics were minimalist, "lightweight" and including the color teal. As a yogini, I happily accepted.

The goal for her personal website was to use less ornamentation, referring to quick and clear calls for action. This allowed for the content — posts, quotes, videos — to stand out. The company website took on similar qualities but a different structure. I worked with a developer to connect the front-end of both sites to a CMS.
