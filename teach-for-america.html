Teach For America

I contracted with Teach for America as the sole web designer (at the time) in their design team. Projects included revamping ~30 web infographics to compliment a global redesign. The largest overhaul was for T.F.A's online store, which was never updated since launch. I provided the art direction, UI, and HTML/CSS of version 2.0. For roughly three months, a project manager and I worked with external developers to integrate the site with their existing CMS.
