Fortune Magazine

When Time Inc. completed its spin-off of Time Warner, I was fortunate to work for and with the smartest editors & writers at Fortune Magazine on many editorial projects under their brand new dot com. It was a mix of static and interactive charts, infographics, prototypes and templates — some of them firsts for the magazine. Below are a handful of many examples.
